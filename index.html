Data Disclaimer

Population figures and trail mileage are approximate estimates compiled from publicly available sources including municipal websites, local trail organizations, and community databases. Values reflect conditions at the time of compilation and may not represent current figures.

Trail mileage typically refers to maintained singletrack network estimates and may include trails on adjacent lands managed by different jurisdictions (national forest, BLM, county open space). City area data uses approximate municipal boundary measurements — small incorporated towns with large surrounding trail systems will show inflated trail-density figures. Population size labels (village, town, city) are informal readability designations only.

This tool is intended for community reference and trail advocacy purposes. Verify all figures with local trail organizations and municipal planning departments before citing in formal documents, grant applications, or public presentations.
